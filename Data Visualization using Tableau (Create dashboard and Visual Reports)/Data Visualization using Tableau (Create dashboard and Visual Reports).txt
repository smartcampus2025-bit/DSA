Example CSV: sales_data.csv
Columns: Region, Category, Sales, Profit, Order Date

Step.1:Open Tableau Desktop

Step.2:Click Connect → Text File

Step.3:Select sales_data.csv → Open

Step.4:Verify fields in Data Source tab

Step.5:Click Sheet 1

Visual Report 1: Bar Chart (Sales by Region)
Step 6. Drag Region → Columns
Step 7. Drag Sales → Rows
Step 8. Click Show Me → Bar Chart
Step 9. Drag Sales → Label (Marks)
Step 10. Rename sheet: Sales by Region

Visual Report 2: Pie Chart (Sales by Category)
Step 11. Click New Worksheet
Step 12. Drag Category → Color
Step 13. Drag Sales → Angle
Step 14. Click Show Me → Pie Chart
Step 15. Rename sheet: Sales by Category

Visual Report 3: Line Chart (Sales Trend)
Step 16. Click New Worksheet
Step 17. Drag Order Date → Columns
Step 18. Drag Sales → Rows
Step 19. Click Show Me → Line Chart
Step 20. Rename sheet: Sales Trend

Add Filter Example
Step 21. Drag Region → Filters
Step 22. Select required regions → OK

Create Dashboard
Step 23. Click New Dashboard
Step 24. Drag Sales by Region, Sales by Category, Sales Trend into dashboard
Step 25. Arrange charts properly
Step 26. Add Dashboard Title
Step 27. Save (File → Save)
